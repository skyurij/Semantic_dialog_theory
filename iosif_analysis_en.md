# Cognitive Analysis: Lesson with Iosif  
*(English translation)*

This analysis describes the meaning‑dynamic synchrony between the teacher and Iosif.  
Iosif demonstrates a structural–intuitive cognitive type, characterized by internal visual modeling,  
non‑linear reasoning, and sensitivity to invariant structures.

---

## ⭐ Summary of Synchrony

**Overall semantic synchrony: 65–70%.**

This means:

- most of the time the teacher and student move in the same direction,  
- but Iosif frequently “falls out of phase”,  
- and the teacher must re‑establish shared context.

This is typical for gifted children with non‑standard cognitive organization.
